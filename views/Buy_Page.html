GiveNTake


On this website, you can do exactly what the website title suggests.

Take:

At this website, you can find clothes that people would no longer like to wear for various reasons. At the moment, the website offers you to buy from 3 categories.
This includes Shoes, Hoodies and Jackets, and Shirts.
You can add the item that you would like to purchase by clicking on the '+' button and the item will go to the cart.
As you may know, when you purchase the clothes, a percentage of that money will be going to charity. This is decided by the person selling the specific item.

Give:

If you click the Sell button on the navbar, it will take you to a page where you can sell items of your choice. There are 3 forms on the Sell page, these correspond with the 3 categories that this website allows at the moment. Further instructions on how to sell clothes will be on that page.
